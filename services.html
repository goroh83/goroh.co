Services

Here you can read about what i do and how I work. If you have any questions feel free to write me…

contact me


Every project is different but I can give you one framework for them all:

We talk.

I listen to your needs. I get to know your business, target(audience) and goals. We determine the function of the website I will design.

Research.

I study your field. In order to design a great website, I must know what your potential customers/visitors are looking for. I also get familiar with your competition. All these things will help me to create your optimal design.

Design.

I start working on a website sketch. Everything from the layout, graphics, to the smallest details like typography and color palette must be well-thought-out. This is what I really love doing- putting the details together for the consummate final product!

Complete project.

We finalize the project. After approving the final look we put the website on-line. From now on the page works for you and your business.

Services details

Below you will find more detailed description of my services.


visual design

Designs from scratch. Depending on your business/brand/target I will prepare concepts, from which we decide the best way forward.


Design includes thorough preparation of layout, colors and typography.

RWD

Responsive web design
The number of people browsing the net on mobile devices is growing rapidly… Your page must look great on all screen sizes, hence it must be up to standards.


Google search engines prioritize user-friendly websites. If your site is not up to modern standards it will be pushed down in the ranks.

maintenace

I update/upgrade your existing content.
I will redesign your site according to current trends and your preferences. If you have new material you want to put on-line I will take care of that too.


If your current site is simply old I can fix that. It’s not only about the looks but also about security and safety…

wordpress

I will set up the whole thing for you. I can prepare and customize existing templates/themes according to your preferences. You don’t have to delve into any details of coding or customizing . You get the final website up and running…
WordPress is most suitable option for most people. You can learn how to change content of website, write new blog posts etc.

support

I will look after your website and its content. Plug-ins need updating, content goes out of date, pictures get boring… For a monthly fee* you have me on stand-by.


* fees determined individually depending on work volume and time required.

SEO

Search engine optimization. In order to end up high in google’s search rank there is a lot of work to be done. SEO is setting up your page with keywords, tags and descriptions so google ‘likes’ it.
Another thing is making your page run faster. If it’s taking too long to load your page, your potential clients will simply close it and move next search result.

domain

To get your very own website you need a domain and hosting suitable for your needs. Not everyone needs expensive plans with big transfers, high speeds and other services.


If you’re not interested in learning those things I will do it for you…

photography

Commercial photography in Columbia, Baltimore, and the greater DC area- available in near future,

You can check some of my work here: photo.goroh.co
